The commercialisation workflow. Tell us where it's wrong.

Before we assess AI and digital tools for research commercialisation, the workflow has to be right. Below is a working map of how research becomes value, drawn from practice, not theory. Mark what is missing, where work stalls, and what your team already uses. Your corrections shape a practical, vendor-neutral map of the sector's real workflow and the tools inside it.

Vendor-neutral by design. Any tools linked to our own network will be assessed against the same criteria as every other candidate. This work will not recommend a purchase. Please do not send confidential, personal or commercially sensitive information.
What comes back to contributors A corrected commercialisation workflow, a vendor-neutral map of tools and internal initiatives in real use, and practical pros and cons. Findings are shared with contributors first. Contributions before 30 September 2026 shape the first synthesis.
