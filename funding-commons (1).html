The Funding Commons

A first-cheque map of UK deep-tech and spinout funding. Grants, programmes and investors are plotted by technology readiness and entry ticket, and classified by the question that actually matters: is this money reachable from where you stand?

The Funding Commons concept dates from the early 2010s. A first public version was built in 2017. This is a clean, independent rebuild for 2026.

Method. Sources are classified by accessibility: open national, open regional, non-dilutive or programme, university-tethered, public co-investor, and later stage. Ticket bands and TRL ranges are indicative entry points, not underwriting rules; always check the live scheme. Cohort standing (front-runner, second-wave) reflects the author’s own analysis of UK spinout deal activity, alongside published sources including the RAEng and Dealroom Spotlight on Spinouts 2026 and the Hickson review. Corrections are welcome: the map improves when practitioners argue with it.
